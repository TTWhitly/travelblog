About Me

Ava Thompson

Hi, I'm Ava Thompson and welcome to my travel blog!

I'm a travel writer and photographer with a passion for exploring the world's hidden gems.

After cancelling my 9-to-5 job to travel full-time, I've spent the last few years discovering new

places, meeting incredible people and capturing the beauty of each destination.

On this blog I share my honest reviews about all my journeys. From the bustling streets of

cities to peaceful corners of nature, my goal is to share the joy of travel and help you discover

all the hidden secrets about the world.
